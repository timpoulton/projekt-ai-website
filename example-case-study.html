The Challenge

Club77's marketing team spent 20+ hours weekly manually editing DJ recordings, writing social captions and scheduling multi-channel posts. This bottleneck limited show promotion and reduced organic reach.

Our Automated Solution

We built a unified pipeline that: ingested raw audio > AI-tagged tracks > auto-generated highlight reels > produced platform-specific captions > scheduled publication via Meta & TikTok APIs — all triggered by a single Google Drive drop.
